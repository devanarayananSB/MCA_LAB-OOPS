Design Team Protocol I: Confidentiality & Ownership

All creative assets, concepts, drafts, and strategic materials shared within the design team are confidential. No work may be redistributed, reused, or disclosed without explicit authorization.

Protocol II: Creative Integrity

Design decisions must align with approved brand standards, visual systems, and project scope. Unauthorized deviations, speculative work, or unapproved revisions are prohibited.

Protocol III: Communication Standards

All feedback, revisions, and approvals must be documented through official communication channels. Verbal approvals are not considered binding.

Protocol IV: Delivery & Version Control

Final assets must be delivered in agreed formats, clearly labeled, and version-controlled. The design team is responsible for maintaining organized archives.

Protocol V: Professional Conduct

All collaborators are expected to maintain professional behavior, respect deadlines, and uphold the financial and reputational integrity of the project.
